☪ DailyDeen - Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

DailyDeen ("we", "our", or "the app") is a mobile application developed by S.U. Digital. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

DailyDeen requests access to your device's GPS location solely to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction based on your geographic position. Your location data is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • Never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Never stored permanently — it is used only in real-time

1.2 Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only using AsyncStorage:

  • Prayer completion tracking (which prayers you marked as prayed)
  • Notification preferences (sound, vibration, or off for each prayer)
  • Calculation method preference
  • Quran bookmarks, favorites, notes, and reading history

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

1.3 Advertising Data

DailyDeen uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect certain data as described in Google's Privacy Policy, including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • General location (derived from IP, not GPS)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

We do not control the data collected by Google AdMob. Please refer to Google's privacy policy for details on how they handle your data.

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Location: To calculate prayer times and Qibla direction
  • Local storage: To remember your preferences and progress
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads and support the app's development

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any third parties, except:

  • Google AdMob: As described above, for advertising purposes
  • Quran API (api.quran.com): The app fetches Quran text from this public API. No personal data is sent — only the surah/ayah number requested

4. Children's Privacy

DailyDeen is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app does not require account creation or any personal information input.

5. Data Security

All user data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any servers that store user data. The security of your data depends on the security of your device.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Deny location access: You can deny or revoke location permission at any time through your device settings. The app will still work, but prayer times and Qibla will not be available.
  • Reset advertising ID: You can reset your advertising ID or opt out of personalized ads through your device settings.
  • Delete local data: You can clear all app data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings.

7. Third-Party Services

DailyDeen uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — for advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Expo / React Native — app framework (no data collection)
  • api.quran.com — for Quran text content (no personal data sent)

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this page periodically.
